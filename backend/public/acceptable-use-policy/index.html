Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules and guidelines for using the MoonLaunch platform. By using MoonLaunch, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account.

1. Prohibited Activities

You must not use MoonLaunch to engage in any of the following:

  • Fraud & Deception: Launching honeypot tokens, rug pulls, or any token designed to deceive or defraud other users.
  • Market Manipulation: Wash trading, pump-and-dump schemes, coordinated price manipulation, or any activity intended to artificially influence token prices.
  • Money Laundering: Using MoonLaunch to launder money or conceal the proceeds of criminal activity.
  • Illegal Activities: Any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international laws or regulations.
  • Impersonation: Creating tokens that impersonate legitimate projects, companies, or individuals without authorisation.
  • Spam & Abuse: Sending unsolicited communications, spamming other users, or abusing platform features.
  • Hacking & Exploits: Attempting to exploit vulnerabilities in the platform, smart contracts, or third-party services.
  • Harmful Content: Uploading or sharing content that is hateful, threatening, harassing, defamatory, or otherwise harmful.
  • Sanctions Violations: Using MoonLaunch if you are located in, or acting on behalf of, any person or entity in a sanctioned country or on a sanctions list.

2. Token Launch Guidelines

When launching a token on MoonLaunch, you agree that:

  • The token does not infringe on any third-party intellectual property rights.
  • The token is not designed to mislead, defraud, or harm other users.
  • You will not artificially manipulate your token's liquidity or price after launch.
  • You accept full responsibility for the token you create and its impact on other users.

3. Wallet & Account Security

You are solely responsible for:

  • Maintaining the security of your wallet and private keys.
  • All activity that occurs through your wallet address on MoonLaunch.
  • Ensuring your account credentials are not shared with or accessible to unauthorised individuals.

MoonLaunch will never ask for your private key or seed phrase. Do not share this information with anyone.

4. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any user or token that violates this Acceptable Use Policy, please report it to us immediately at moonlaunchapp@gmail.com. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

5. Enforcement

MoonLaunch reserves the right to:

  • Remove or restrict access to any token or content that violates this policy.
  • Suspend or permanently ban any user who violates this policy.
  • Report illegal activity to relevant law enforcement authorities.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Continued use of MoonLaunch after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this policy, contact us at: moonlaunchapp@gmail.com
